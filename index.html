Welcome to Eduardo Marschall's first webpage

This is a part of The Odin Project. I was tasked with creating a website in which I could exercise all the things I have learned so far in regards to HTML. I added two of my favorite recipes here The first one is a recipe that is close to my heart, since my mom used to make waffles all the time for me and my siblings. The way we do it, though, is not as people usually do in the USA. We are not used to have is as a main breakfast component, we usually make a big batch of waffles and have them from time to time for breakfast, afternoon coffee, or even after lunch. We usually freeze a big portion of what we make and defrost it from time to time.

The second recipe is something that I have always enjoyed doing, I learned how to make caipirinhas while I was dating my first boyfriend. After I got the gist of it, I started to get more adventurous, making caipirinhas with all sorts of fruits, like strawberries, pineapple, cashew, passion fruit and many other combinations. This became "my thing" and I liked being the "barman" of our small parties. I still like making them and I tend to be in charge of doing that in our gatherings, but I don't drink as much anymore.

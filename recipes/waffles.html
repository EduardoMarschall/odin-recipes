Waffles

Main Page

Description

One of the most beloved breakfast all over the world is not as complicated to prepare as you might think! Follow along and try to prepare these delicious little pieces of heaven!

Ingredients

  • 10 Eggs
  • 4 tablespoons of Butter or Margarine
  • 1 Kg of Flour
  • 3 cups of Sugar
  • 1 Litter of Milk
  • 4 teaspoons of Baking Powder
  • Zest of 4 Limes

Steps

  1. Add all the liquid ingredients to a mixer
  2. Slowly add flour, sugar and baking powder
  3. Add the Lime Zest and mix everything until the mixture is homogeneous
  4. Heat up your waffle machine, add some butter or vegetable oil to the iron and start baking some Waffles!
  5. Enjoy your waffles with some honey, marmalade, mapple syrup, or with some fresh fruits (yummy)
